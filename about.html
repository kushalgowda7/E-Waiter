About us

The E-waiter project is a web-based application that revolutionizes the traditional dining experience by providing a contactless and efficient ordering system for customers in our restaurant. With this, customers can browse through the menu, select their desired items and customize their orders without interacting with the waiter. This ensures that customer orders and preferences are processed quickly and accurately. We deliver high quality service and personalized dining experiences to the customers.


Benefits of this application

  • To provide a contactless and efficient ordering system for customers in our restaurant

  • To reduce the workload of the waitstaff, minimize errors in taking orders, and increase table turnover.

  • To provide a user-friendly interface for customers to browse through the menu, select their desired items, and customize their orders based on their preferences.

  • To improve the efficiency and profitability of our restaurant by streamlining their operations, enhancing customer satisfaction, and reducing operational costs.
